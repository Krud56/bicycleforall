About Us

Cycle For All is an web-based platform to avail on-demand bicycle/e-bike rentals in select Indian locations.
The company provides GPS and Bluetooth enabled bicycles& e-bike for rent via a mobile app.
The user can view nearby available bicycles on the aweb, scan the QR code on the bicycle to unlock it and ride.
Users are required to park the vehicle in designated 'Cycle zones' to end the rental.
Its mobile app is available for iOS and Android devices.
